Jetok - TikTok Follower Analyzer

This personal tool allows users to connect their TikTok account and retrieve their own list of followers using the official Research API. The data is analyzed and categorized based on each follower's reach. No data is stored or shared.

How it works

  1. You log in using TikTok’s secure OAuth2 process.
  2. The app fetches your public follower list (using Research API).
  3. You receive a local CSV file with categorized follower metrics.
